## Welcome

The **Thermal Adaptation & Vector Biology Lab** investigates how environmental temperature shapes
physiology, fitness, and molecular adaptation in disease‑vector insects, with a focus on
*Anopheles gambiae* across Nigerian ecological zones.

### Research Focus
- Thermal tolerance and stress physiology
- Fitness trade‑offs under heat stress
- Molecular and biochemical mechanisms
- Climate change and malaria transmission
